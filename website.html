You make progress by implementing ideas."-Shirley Chisholm. That's why every bunch of students are put together in a team to find a solution for one of these challenges, whether you like it or not,you have to accept your fate and work extremely hard to come up with a project that is based on a scientific research. No one cares if you have been to venus, studied the aliens in mars or travelled through time and had a conversation with Darwin unless it's SCIENTIFIC
